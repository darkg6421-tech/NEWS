Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

📋 Information We Collect

NewsHub respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best news experience:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with our app (pages visited, features used)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and operating system for optimization
  • Saved Articles: Articles you choose to save are stored locally on your device
  • Preferences: Your theme preference and category selections

🔒 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our news service
  • To personalize your news feed based on your interests
  • To improve and optimize our application performance
  • To remember your preferences and saved articles

📱 Local Storage & Data

All your saved articles, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not upload this data to any server. This means:

  • Your saved articles remain private to your device
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your saved articles
  • We cannot access your saved articles remotely

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality (theme preference)
  • Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve our service

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • News API: We fetch news from Google News RSS feed. Please review Google's Privacy Policy
  • External Links: News articles link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices

👶 Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

🛡️ Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers
  • Regular security updates and best practices

📧 Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@newshub.com
  • Through our Contact Form

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (check browser's localStorage)
  • Delete your saved articles (use the app's remove feature)
  • Opt-out of analytics (disable in settings)
  • Clear all data by clearing browser storage
